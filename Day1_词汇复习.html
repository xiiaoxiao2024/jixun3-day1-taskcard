Quick Look — Easy Words 快速过一遍（15个）

These words are simple — just read through them once, no need to test yourself. 这些词比较简单，读一遍认识就行，不用测试。

happy (adj.)
开心的
Feeling pleased or glad.
sad (adj.)
伤心的
Feeling unhappy, often because something bad happened.
angry (adj.)
生气的
Feeling strong displeasure or annoyance.
scared (adj.)
害怕的
Feeling afraid of something.
excited (adj.)
兴奋的
Feeling very happy and full of energy about something.
worried (adj.)
担心的
Feeling nervous about something that might go wrong.
surprised (adj.)
惊讶的
Feeling shocked because something unexpected happened.
proud (adj.)
自豪的
Feeling pleased about something you or someone else has done well.
kind (adj.)
善良的
Friendly, caring, and helpful to others.
mean (adj.)
刻薄的
Unkind or unfriendly to others.
silly (adj.)
傻乎乎的
Funny in a foolish or playful way.
tired (adj.)
疲倦的
Needing rest or sleep; feeling low on energy.
brave (adj.)
勇敢的
Willing to face danger or difficulty without being too afraid.
shy (adj.)
害羞的
Nervous or uncomfortable around other people.
calm (adj.)
平静的
Not worried, angry, or excited; peaceful.
